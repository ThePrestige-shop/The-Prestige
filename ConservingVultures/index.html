JOIN INTERNATIONAL VULTURE AWARENESS DAY (IVAD) 2025 CELEBRATION

Vultures have their special day, too. The International Vulture Awareness Day is celebrated every year on the first Saturday in September. For 2025, the day falls on September 6, 2025. As part of activities to celebrate the day in Ghana, the Rufford Foundation is supporting an awareness campaign for small and large scale stakeholders. Which means YOU! Come join us to celebrate and share the importance of vultures with all.

To learn more, visit the website link or WhatsApp us on how you can join and contribute.

Information

This website (www.conservingvultures.org) is dedicated to protecting vulture species, with emphasis now on West African vultures. We are based in Ghana and contribute our quota to conserve the six predominant vulture species in Ghana (pictured above). This website is possible through funding from the "Rufford Foundation."
