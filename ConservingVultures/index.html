How You Can Help

Change Perception

Vultures are not evil. They protect public health by removing carcasses and preventing disease spread. Help correct harmful myths.

Confront Persecution

Report egg theft, poisoning, or illegal hunting. Silence allows extinction. Collective vigilance protects nesting populations.

Support Research

Support conservation organizations working on vulture ecology, awareness, and policy change across Africa.

International Vulture Awareness Day 2025

IVAD is celebrated annually on the first Saturday in September. In 2025, it falls on September 6, 2025.

Join stakeholders across Ghana and beyond in raising awareness about the ecological importance of vultures. Supported by The Rufford Foundation.
